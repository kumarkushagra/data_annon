>>> Defination

0. plastic
Any plastic item: bottles, bags, wrappers, containers, broken plastic parts.

1. paper_cardboard
Paper, newspapers, books, cartons, boxes, paper packaging.

2. metal
Metal objects such as cans, foil, tins, scrap metal pieces.

3. glass
Glass items including bottles, jars, broken glass fragments.

4. organic_food
Food waste: leftovers, peels, vegetable waste, rotten food.

5. textile
Cloth-based items: clothes, rags, fabric pieces, shoes.

6. rubber
Rubber materials: tires, footwear soles, rubber bands, hoses.

7. wood
Wooden waste: sticks, planks, furniture parts, plywood.

8. e_waste
Electronic waste: cables, chargers, circuit boards, devices.

9. hazardous
Dangerous waste: batteries, syringes, chemicals, sharp objects.
